Qrtick: Fundraiser Guide

How We Support Your Mission

We handle all the technical details so you can focus on your cause:

  1. Quick Setup: Your fundraiser will be ready in 5 business days
  2. Local Support: Our Jamaica-based team configures everything to your needs
  3. Payment Setup: Direct integration with your local bank account
  4. Testing: We verify all systems before your tickets go live
  5. Launch Ready: Full support as you start selling tickets

Managing Your Fundraiser

Event Setup

  • Simple event details: name, description, date, and location
  • Optional event banner image to showcase your cause
  • Custom branding available for larger fundraisers

Ticket Options

  • Create different ticket levels for various donor categories
  • Set different prices for early-bird, regular, and VIP supporters
  • Control how many tickets are available
  • Easy to share via WhatsApp and email
  • Color-coded tickets for easy identification
  • Digital tickets that work on any phone

Check-in System

  • Works on any smartphone - no special equipment needed
  • Scan tickets even without internet connection
  • Track attendance in real-time
  • Multiple team members can check in guests simultaneously
  • Simple backup system if someone forgets their ticket
  • Everything syncs when internet connection returns

Launch Checklist

Payment Setup

  • Confirm your bank account details
  • Test the payment system
  • Verify deposits are working
  • Set up refund process (if needed)

Event Details

  • Double-check event name and description
  • Verify date and time
  • Confirm venue details
  • Review ticket prices and types
  • Set ticket limits
  • Test promotional codes (if using)

Team Preparation

  • Set up check-in team access
  • Test ticket scanning
  • Practice offline mode
  • Review backup check-in process
