How We Support Your Mission

  3. Payment Setup: Direct integration with your local bank account
  4. Testing: We verify all systems before your tickets go live
  5. Launch Ready: Full support as you start selling tickets

  • Optional event banner image to showcase your cause
  • Custom branding available for larger fundraisers

Ticket Options

  • Create different ticket levels for various donor categories
  • Set different prices for early-bird, regular, and VIP supporters
  • Control how many tickets are available
  • Easy to share via WhatsApp and email

  • Multiple team members can check in guests simultaneously

How Sales Work

Event Listing

  • Your event appears on your custom ticket page once published
  • Real-time ticket count shows how many are still available
  • Events automatically show as "SOLD OUT" when all tickets are gone
  • System prevents overselling by tracking inventory in real-time

Purchase Process

  1. Supporter clicks your ticket link or finds your event on tickets.qrtick.com
  2. They select ticket type and quantity
  3. Enter their email address
  4. Complete payment
  5. Receive confirmation email with QR-coded tickets
Pro Tip: Each order gets a unique ID (e.g., "7630001ab") that helps track purchase details and quantity.

Checking In Attendees

Using Your Phone to Scan

  • Open Thing in your phone's web browser
  • Log in with your registered email
  • Scan QR codes from printed tickets or phone displays
  • Get instant validation results
  • System prevents duplicate check-ins
  • Works even without internet connection

Manual Check-in Options

  • Search by ticket number if QR code won't scan
  • Look up attendees by name
  • Works offline with automatic sync when connection returns
  • Quick access to all ticket details

Real-time Dashboard

  • See how many guests have arrived
  • Track multiple check-in points
  • Monitor staff activity
  • Get alerts for any issues (duplicates, invalid tickets)
  • Export attendance data after the event

Promoting Your Ticket Page

We make it easy to share your ticket page with potential supporters:

  • Use your custom direct link (e.g., tickets.qrtick.com/your-event-name)
  • Or simply tell supporters to visit tickets.qrtick.com and search for your event
  • Share your link directly through WhatsApp, email, or social media
  • Your ticket page works perfectly on both mobile phones and computers
  • Supporters can purchase multiple tickets in one transaction
