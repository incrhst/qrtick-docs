How Sales Work

Event Listing

  • Your event appears on your custom ticket page once published
  • Real-time ticket count shows how many are still available
  • Events automatically show as "SOLD OUT" when all tickets are gone
  • System prevents overselling by tracking inventory in real-time

Purchase Process

  1. Supporter clicks your ticket link or finds your event on tickets.qrtick.com
  2. They select ticket type and quantity
  3. Enter their email address
  4. Complete payment
  5. Receive confirmation email with QR-coded tickets
Pro Tip: Each order gets a unique ID (e.g., "7630001ab") that helps track purchase details and quantity.

Checking In Attendees with the QRTick Scanner App (Android)

Our dedicated Scanner App (currently available for Android) provides a robust and reliable way to manage check-ins at your event, even without a stable internet connection.

Getting Started

  • Secure Login: Log in with the email and password associated with your QRTick account.
  • Event Selection: After login, you'll see a list of events you're authorized to scan for. Select the current event to begin. Event banner images are shown for easy identification.

Key Feature: Robust Offline Mode

One of the core strengths of the scanner app is its ability to function reliably offline:

  • Data Sync: Before the event (while online), sync the event data. This downloads all ticket information (including validity status) to the device.
  • Offline Scanning: Scan tickets seamlessly without an internet connection. The app validates tickets against the locally stored data.
  • Local Storage: Check-ins performed while offline are securely saved on the device.
  • Automatic Sync: When network connectivity returns, the app automatically syncs the offline check-ins with the server in the background.
  • Offline Indicator: A clear visual indicator lets you know when the app is operating in offline mode.
  • Manual Refresh: You can manually trigger a data re-sync when you regain connectivity if needed.

Real-time QR Code Scanning

  • Fast Detection: Uses the device camera for quick QR code scanning.
  • Optimized Scanning: Ignores small or distant QR codes to prevent accidental scans.

Instant Ticket Validation (Online & Offline)

Get immediate feedback on every scan:

  • Checks Performed: Verifies ticket existence, event match, previous check-in status (locally, pending sync, or on the server), and invalid/refunded status.
