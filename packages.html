Payout Rules

  • Available only on Professional and Elite packages.
  • Minimum payout amount: $50,000 JMD.
  • Minimum fee per early payout request: $5,000 JMD.
  • Maximum request: up to 75% of current available balance.
  • Limit: Maximum 2 early payout requests per event.
