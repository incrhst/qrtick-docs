# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Jamaican event organisers, fundraisers, and production companies looking to set up digital ticketing and manage their gate entry.

## Product Purpose
QRTick provides professional, Jamaica-focused digital ticketing and event management solutions, handling setup to check-in, with direct bank deposits in JMD.

## Brand Personality
Professional, local (Jamaican event focus), reliable, and straightforward.

## Anti-references
SaaS cliché layouts: tiny uppercase tracked eyebrows above every section, numbered display markers as scaffolding, or overly aggressive saturated grays/gradients.

## Design Principles
- Direct and Helpful: Speak clearly to organizers without jargon or unnecessary marketing buzzwords.
- Reliable and Local: Convey expert local support and straightforward processes tailored to the Jamaican event context.
- Readable and Restrained: High readability, comfortable whitespace, and clean typography that doesn't shout.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance for high contrast, clean typography for reading guides, and supportive layouts for mobile gate staff.
