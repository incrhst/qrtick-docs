Event Organizer Checklist

Use this comprehensive checklist to ensure smooth event check-in operations. We recommend reviewing this list at least one week before your event.

Pre-Event Setup

  • Ensure all team members have downloaded the QRTick app on their devices
  • Verify all team members can log in to their accounts
  • Test internet connectivity at the venue
  • Prepare backup power banks for all devices
  • Print emergency contact information for key team members

Device Management Best Practices

  • Download event data on all devices while online before the event
  • Assign specific entry points or ticket types to different devices (e.g., one phone for VIP, another for general admission)
  • Regularly sync devices during the event if Wi-Fi or cellular data is available
  • Monitor the attendee list in the app to confirm check-ins are accurately recorded
  • Test the multi-device setup beforehand for high-volume events

For events with multiple entry points, create a simple map showing where each team member should be positioned. This helps prevent confusion and ensures all entrances are properly staffed.

Day-of-Event Checklist

  • Arrive at least 2 hours before doors open
  • Test all devices one final time
  • Conduct a brief team meeting to review assignments
  • Set up clear signage for different entry points
  • Establish a central command point for troubleshooting
üîã Device Preparation

Make sure all devices are fully charged and bring backup power banks. For events longer than 4 hours, we recommend having at least one backup device ready.

Post-Event Tasks

  • Ensure all devices have synced their final check-in data
  • Review the attendance statistics
  • Document any issues or suggestions for improvement
  • Back up event data if needed
  • Schedule a brief team debrief if necessary
üìä Data Verification

Always verify that your final check-in numbers match your expected attendance. If there are discrepancies, review the check-in logs while the event is still fresh in everyone's mind.
